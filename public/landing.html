When should I plant tomatoes?
In a Western Europe maritime climate, start tomato seedlings indoors in February–March. Transplant outdoors after your last frost, typically mid to late May. A greenhouse or polytunnel gives the most reliable crop. Space 50cm apart, full sun, water deeply every 2–3 days.
What about companions?
Great companions for tomatoes: basil (repels aphids), carrots (loosen soil), marigolds (nematode control). Avoid planting near fennel or brassicas.
