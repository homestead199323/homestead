FAQ

Fair questions.

I've never grown anything. Is this for me?

You're exactly who it's built for. MyTerra assumes zero knowledge: it tells you what to do, when, and why — in plain words, one small job at a time. If you can follow a game quest, you can follow a carrot bed.

Does it really work offline?

Yes. The whole app — tasks, guides, maps, logs — runs with no connection. Changes queue up and sync to your cloud backup the moment you're back online.

Which regions does it cover?

The base data is tuned for Western Europe's maritime climate (UK, Ireland, Benelux, northern France). Regional overrides adapt planting windows and varieties for Northern Europe, the Mediterranean, and warm and cold zones of the US & Canada.

Phone or laptop?

Both. MyTerra runs in any modern browser and installs to your phone's home screen like a native app. Your farm syncs across every device you sign in on.

What happens after the 7 days?

You pick a plan to keep going — Basic, Pro, or the one-time Lifetime deal. No card is taken up front, so nothing charges automatically. Your farm data stays safe either way.
