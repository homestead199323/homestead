When should I plant tomatoes?
In your Mediterranean zone (9b), start tomato seedlings indoors in February. Transplant outdoors after last frost, typically mid-March to early April. Choose heat-tolerant varieties like San Marzano for Albanian summers. Space 45-60cm apart, full sun, water deeply 2-3x per week.
What about companions?
Great companions for tomatoes: basil (repels aphids), carrots (loosen soil), marigolds (nematode control). Avoid planting near fennel or brassicas.
